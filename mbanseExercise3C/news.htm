Pga Tour News

Fedex Cup Update

ATLANTA--Jordan Spieth, who came in ranked second, leads the playoff finale at 8 under, one stroke ahead of Henrik Stenson, the 2013 FedExCup champ who arrived in Atlanta fourth in the standings.

The Swede led after each of the first two days but Spieth is the only player with three sub-par rounds and a birdie at the 18th hole on Saturday to separate the two.

"I'm very motivated," said Spieth, who counts the Masters and the U.S. Open among his four wins in 2015. "It's something I want badly, just, and it's going to feel a lot like a major championship when I step on the first tee."
